We offer the following service & installations.

FIBER INTERNET

Fiber optic cables are used to transmit data at a speed of over 100 Megabits per second. It is highly reliable with no signal degradation thus ensuring fast internet speeds 24/7. Fiber connections are currently available in Nyeri Town, Kangemi, Skuta, Pembe Tatu & Othaya Town. Other locations to be announced soon. All fiber installations are free of charge. A router is leased to our clients at no extra cost.

WIRELESS INTERNET

Wireless Internet Installation is free but a range of devices are required to make te connection possible. Our client incur the cost of buying the equipment which may be purchased from the company. Receiver prices vary from Kshs.5500-Kshs.7500, depending on the type(location affects the type of receive to be used). Wireless routers cost Kshs. 2500. Wireless Internet is available in almost all locations in the Central Region of Kenya

CCTV Installation

We offer quality CCTV installations at fair prices for homes,schools,offices & other site in any given location.
